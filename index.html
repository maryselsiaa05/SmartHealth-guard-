Heart Rate Information

The system monitors pulse rate using photoplethysmography (PPG) principles. It calculates Beats Per Minute (BPM) and Pulse Frequency (Hz).

Below 60 BPM – Bradycardia

60 – 90 BPM – Normal

90 – 120 BPM – Elevated

Above 120 BPM – Tachycardia
